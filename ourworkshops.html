Our Programs

In order to achieve the above objectives for the benefit of all stakeholders involved in the tourism and hospitality sector in the western coast, we have developed a series of workshops. These programmes are carefully thought through to give the wanderers in Negombo a novel and immersive experience. The sessions are curated in a format of training workshop and performance, so that the wanderer gets to participate and engage in the sessions rather than only being a member in the audience.


Social Dancing for Absolute Beginners

Foster a circle of Social Dance enthusiasts in Negombo, by promoting and conducting dance related workshops and programmes.

Paint Your Ocean

Dive into creativity with our "Paint Your Ocean" workshop. Capture the beauty of the sea on canvas with the guidance of local artists. We offer this experience where all skill levels are welcome!

Paint The Lagoon

Immerse yourself in the shifting colors and light of the lagoon at dusk. Add a moment of aesthetic therapy to your travel experience with a serene, colorful lagoon evening. We invite guests to join our "Paint Your Lagoon" workshop for an unforgettable experience.

Poetry Night

A signature event that phases sublime contemplative thoughts with the priceless works of the world's greatest poets, creating an experience filled with an unprecedented sense of Sri Lanka for our travelling friends.

Shanthikarma

We offer a unique tourism wellness experience with Sri Lanka's healing art, a jewel of Asian healing arts. This opportunity allows our tourists to discover the beauty and hidden vision of these rare traditions.

Puppetry In Sri Lanka

Imagine yourself surrounded by the rhythmic beats of traditional drums, the colorful costumes of skilled puppeteers, and the enchanting movements of intricately crafted puppets. In 'Rukada' performances, ancient stories and myths come to life through the artful manipulation of puppets, creating an immersive experience that captivates senses and stirs your imagination.

Performance Arts

A unique and engaging performance-art show that transcends conventional boundaries. This provides an opportunity to experience the combination of visual arts.

Toddy Tour

Negombo's elixir, a unique drink derived from the coconut flower: A conversational journey in search of the rare Toddy. A day to enjoy the beauty of Negombo's unique culture with a drink.

Architectural Walk

An interactive tour to explore Negombo's ancient architectural wonders and cultural landscape. This experience is a celebration of the intellectual and aesthetic dimensions of architecture.
