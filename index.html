WELCOME TO WORKXOP!

Welcome to WORKXOP, your gateway to the cultural and artistic heart of Negombo. We are passionate advocates for preserving and promoting cultural richness, committed to creating experiences that enrich the lives of both locals and visitors.


At WORKXOP, we have developed a unique initiative through Development Media Network (Pvt.)Ltd., aimed at collaborating with various stakeholders in Sri Lanka's cultural and economic landscape. Our goal is to enhance the visibility and value of their causes, products, and services.


Discover Sri Lanka with WORKXOP

Embark on unforgettable journeys with our carefully curated arts and media programs, celebrating the diverse cultural tapestry of Negombo. Our interdisciplinary workshops offer immersive experiences that showcase the aesthetic beauty and vibrant dynamism of this unique region.


Enrich Your Journey

Nurture your appreciation for cultural heritage through our transformative art and cultural workshops. These programs are designed to provide a deeper understanding and connection to Negombo's unique traditions, fostering meaningful interactions between visitors and the local community.


Join Us

Join us at WORKXOP to discover the vibrant tapestry of Negombo through our curated art and cultural programs. Experience the stories, traditions, and beauty that make Negombo a unique destination, and create lasting memories.

Our Vision

Transforming Negombo into a cultural hub for tourist attractions.

Our Mission

Executing interdisciplinary and media-based interventions to promote the cultural diversity of Negombo.
