Our Programs

In order to achieve the above objectives for the benefit of all stakeholders involved in the tourism and hospitality sector in the western coast, we have developed a series of workshops.

These programmes are carefully thought through to give the wanderers in Negombo a novel and immersive experience. The sessions are curated in a format of training workshop and performance, so that the wanderer gets to participate and engage in the sessions rather than only being a member in the audience.


Social Dancing for Absolute Beginners

Hosting dance workshops to build a social dance community in Negombo, encouraging participation through engaging programs and events.

Paint The Lagoon

Immerse yourself in twilight lagoon colors. Add an aesthetic touch to your trip with a peaceful evening. Join our "Paint Your Lagoon" workshop for a truly memorable experience.

Paint Your Ocean

Dive into creativity with our "Paint Your Ocean" workshop. Capture the beauty of the sea on canvas with the guidance of local artists. We offer this experience where all skill levels are welcome!

Shanthikarma

Discover Sri Lanka’s unique tourism wellness experience through healing arts, a jewel of Asian tradition. Tourists can explore the beauty and hidden visions of these rare, ancient practices.

Culinary Journey

Join our well-organized cooking sessions that showcase Negombo’s distinctive fish flavors. Creative chefs prepare amazing dishes using world-famous fish from Sri Lanka’s oceans, lagoons, and rivers for tourists.

Poetry Night - Kahlil

Experience sublime contemplative thoughts paired with the priceless works of Kahlil, one of the world’s greatest poets. This signature event creates an unprecedented sense of Sri Lanka for our traveling friends.

Poetry Night - Neruda

Experience sublime contemplative thoughts paired with the priceless works of Neruda, one of the world’s greatest poets. This signature event creates an unprecedented sense of Sri Lanka for our traveling friends.

Sri Lankan Jazz Nights

Indulge in an elegant Dance Evening featuring Sri Lanka Jazz. Enjoy a magical night filled with musicianship, creating endless unforgettable moments that thrill the hearts of tourists!

Coconut Toddy Tour

Explore Negombo's unique culture through its elixir, a drink derived from the coconut flower. Enjoy a conversational journey in search of the rare Toddy, a local treasure.

Negombo City Tour

Join a conversational city tour revealing why Negombo is known as "Little Rome." Guided by experienced archaeologists, this exploration offers memorable insights into the city's unique heritage.

Puppetry In Sri Lanka

Immerse yourself in 'Rukada' performances, where traditional drums, colorful costumes, and skilled puppeteers bring ancient stories to life. This captivating art form stirs the imagination and engages the senses.

Plate Painting

Explore clay painting techniques while creating a unique souvenir. This engaging experience is perfect for tourists interested in local arts and crafts, providing a memorable creative outlet.

Resin Art

Enjoy a magical experience that immortalizes human memories through art. This series of activities guides travelers from ancient resin techniques to modern chemical innovations, blending tradition with creativity.

Traditional Drum Playing

Experience the traditional drum beats of Sri Lanka, discovering powerful aspects of sound therapy traditionally used to heal mental illnesses. This unique opportunity enhances wellness through ancient practices.

Fire Dance

Witness a thrilling musical and dance experience that blurs the boundary between fire and the human body. Inspired by Sri Lankan healing arts, this event creates unforgettable photographic moments.

Peg Doll Painting

A creative experience where tourists craft peg dolls inspired by Sri Lankan folklore, enhancing cultural connections and personal expression. ​

Traditional Mask Making

An immersive experience where tourists learn the art of traditional Sri Lankan mask making, exploring cultural significance while crafting personalized masks and engaging in storytelling. ​

Traditional Mask Painting

An immersive experience where tourists learn about Sri Lankan mask-making and decorate their own masks, exploring cultural significance and personal storytelling through art. ​

Happiness of Green - “Eco Friendly Crafts”

A hands-on experience where tourists craft sustainable products from pan reeds, connecting with local artisans and exploring Sri Lanka's cultural heritage while promoting eco-friendly tourism.

Discover Negombo's Hidden Gems by Bicycle

Discover Negombo, also known as "Little Rome," it boasts colonial landmarks, serene lagoons, and vibrant markets. Join this bicycle tour to explore its scenic trails offering an unforgettable adventure for all.

Deep Sea Fishing Boat Tour in Negombo

Embark on a thrilling deep-sea fishing tour from Negombo, targeting tuna and more, guided by experts. Enjoy stunning coastal views, marine life, and a safe, enjoyable experience with top-quality gear.
