Jazz Music Night

Immerse your clients in Sri Lankan Jazz Night, an extraordinary addition to the global jazz narrative. This event blends soulful jazz melodies with the distinctive sounds of Sri Lanka, creating a captivating and unique musical experience. Set against the enchanting backdrop of Negombo's serene evening beach, the rhythmic waves and coastal breeze harmonize seamlessly with the music, evoking an unforgettable connection to both the art and the locale.

This is more than a performance—it's an exploration of cultural richness and innovation that rejuvenates the soul and energizes the body’s "jazz cells."

Time Duration:

2 -3 hours

Location:

Negombo, Sri Lanka

Suitable Ages:

Ages 16 & over

Participants:

05 - 40 pax.

01 Session Per Day:

From 18:00h onwards

Highlights:

  • Be surrounded by a beautifully decorated venue adorned with native Sri Lankan flowers and the soothing sounds of the sea.
  • Experience live recitations of Neruda’s famous poems.
  • Engage in dramatic presentations about Neruda's connection to Sri Lanka, featuring vivid storytelling and monologues.
  • Participate in collective poetry recitations and karaoke-style performances with musical improvisation.
  • Enjoy melodies inspired by traditional Sinhala and Chilean tunes from the 60s and 70s
