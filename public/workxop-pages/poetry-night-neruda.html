Poetry Night with Neruda

The Poetry Night with Neruda offers participants a refined and culturally immersive evening dedicated to the works of Pablo Neruda. Set against the serene backdrop of Sri Lanka's coastline, this event combines poetry, music, and storytelling into a thoughtfully curated experience. Guests will explore Neruda’s evocative poems, reflecting themes of the sea, love, and life.

The program features live recitations accompanied by classical music, dramatic presentations highlighting Neruda’s connection to Sri Lanka, and collective readings designed to foster engagement and appreciation. Enhanced by the soothing sounds of the sea and melodies from Sinhala and Chilean tunes, this event provides an elegant platform to connect with the poet’s profound appreciation of coastal life and natural beauty.

Time Duration:

2 hours

Location:

Negombo, Sri Lanka

Suitable Ages:

Ages 18 & Over

Participants:

05 - 20 pax.

01 Session Per Day:

From 17:30h onwards

Highlights:

  • Be surrounded by a beautifully decorated venue adorned with native Sri Lankan flowers and the soothing sounds of the sea.
  • Experience live recitations of Neruda’s famous poems.
  • Engage in dramatic presentations about Neruda's connection to Sri Lanka, featuring vivid storytelling and monologues.
  • Participate in collective poetry recitations and karaoke-style performances with musical improvisation.
  • Enjoy melodies inspired by traditional Sinhala and Chilean tunes from the 60s and 70s.
