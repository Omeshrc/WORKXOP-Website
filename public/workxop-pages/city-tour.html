Unique Negombo City Tour

Negombo, often recognized as the cultural capital of Sri Lanka, is a vibrant coastal city rich in history, spirituality, and local charm. This half-day tour offers an immersive experience, beginning with a visit to the bustling Negombo Fish Market and Kamachchode Market. These marketplaces serve as the heart of the city, showcasing its rich fishing heritage and the daily rhythms of local life.

St. Mary’s Church, with its stunning architecture and serene atmosphere, stands as a testament to the Christian legacy in the region, while the nearby Angurukaramulla Temple captivates visitors with its massive seated Buddha statue and colorful murals depicting Buddhist teachings. Additionally, a visit to the remnants of the Dutch Fort allows you to step back into the colonial era, providing a historical perspective on Negombo’s strategic importance during the Dutch occupation. The tour concludes with a tranquil Dutch Canal tour, meandering through wetlands teeming with birdlife, scenic landscapes, and the calm waters of the Negombo Lagoon, creating a harmonious blend of history, nature, and cultural exploration.
