Puppetry Show

The puppet show provides a fascinating insight into the cultural and artistic traditions of Negombo, a coastal town renowned for its vibrant fishing community. Through the intricate art of puppetry, the performance brings to life stories rooted in the lives of local fishermen—their daily struggles, aspirations, and deep connection with the sea. Reflecting the heartbeat of Negombo’s coastal life, traditional Sri Lankan wooden puppets come alive through a harmonious blend of storytelling and music.

A celebration of both art and heritage, the show offers a unique perspective on the values and traditions that have shaped Negombo’s identity. It highlights the resilience and spirit of its people, along with the artistic skills passed down through generations. The performance serves as a cultural bridge, preserving and sharing the region’s rich history through an engaging and immersive art form. Participants will also have the opportunity to try their hand at puppetry during the show.
