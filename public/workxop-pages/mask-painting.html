Traditional Mask Painting Workshop

Dive into the vibrant world of Sri Lankan art with our Traditional Mask Painting Workshop in Negombo. This unique experience invites you to explore the rich heritage of mask artistry, where tradition meets creativity.

Rooted in the cultural heart of Sri Lanka, these intricately carved wooden masks have long been used in rituals, theater, and healing ceremonies. While Ambalangoda is famed as the birthplace of mask making, Negombo offers its own artistic flair, influenced by its maritime culture and diverse community.

During the workshop, you’ll bring a blank wooden mask to life using vivid colors and traditional techniques, guided by skilled artisans. Learn the meanings behind symbolic patterns and hues while connecting with the folklore and spiritual practices that make Sri Lankan masks iconic.

  • Experience a live traditional dance performance featuring the masks in use.
