Deep Sea Fishing Boat Tour in Negombo

This thrilling deep-sea fishing tour begins in Negombo, where you'll sail 12–15 nautical miles into the Indian Ocean to explore productive fishing grounds. For those targeting larger catches like tuna, the trip extends further, heading towards Colombo or Chilaw based on the best fishing conditions.

Led by expert local fishermen, this tour offers the chance to catch various fish species, including tuna, mackerel, and barracuda. The boat is equipped with high-quality fishing gear and safety equipment, ensuring a safe and enjoyable experience. As you sail deeper into the ocean, enjoy stunning views of Sri Lanka’s coastline and marine life.

This tour is perfect for both experienced anglers and beginners.
