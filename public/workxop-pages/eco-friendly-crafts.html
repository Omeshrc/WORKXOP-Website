Eco Friendly Handicrafts Workshop

An eco-friendly craft experience featuring the traditional art of pan reed weaving, this immersive program invites tourists to connect with local Sri Lankan artisans in Negombo while creating sustainable products that reflect the country’s commitment to preserving its cultural and environmental heritage.

Participants will engage with the artistry of pan reed weaving, a craft passed down through generations in rural communities. Guided by skilled artisans, they will create functional and decorative items such as baskets, mats, and ornaments. The program ensures an unforgettable and meaningful encounter with Sri Lankan tradition by blending cultural education, creative activities, and sustainability.

This initiative preserves the artistry of pan reed weaving by promoting it to an international audience, providing economic opportunities for local artisans, and celebrating their craft. Tourists leave with their own handmade products and a deep connection to Sri Lanka’s rich cultural identity.
