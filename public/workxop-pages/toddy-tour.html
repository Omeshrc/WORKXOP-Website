Toddy Tour: Exploring the "Ra" in Negombo

The Toddy Tour offers an immersive journey into the heart of Sri Lanka's toddy-tapping tradition, a practice deeply rooted in the coastal communities of Negombo. This tour provides participants with a unique opportunity to learn about the rich cultural and historical significance of Ra (toddy), a traditional fermented beverage made from coconut or palmyra sap.

Guests will be guided through various stages of the toddy production process, starting with a warm welcome and an introduction to the history of toddy tapping in Sri Lanka. The tour includes a visit to a coconut grove where local toddy tappers demonstrate the art of collecting sap from coconut trees. Participants will also have the chance to taste freshly tapped toddy alongside traditional Sri Lankan pol roti, enhancing their understanding of the flavors and cultural importance of this unique beverage. The experience is led by knowledgeable guides who provide insights into the historical and cultural context of Ra, making it a perfect blend of education and enjoyment.
