Traditional Mask Making Workshop

Join our Traditional Mask Making Workshop and immerse yourself in the timeless craft of Sri Lankan mask artistry, where culture, history, and creativity come together. While the vibrant tradition of mask making originates from Ambalangoda, this workshop sheds light on Negombo’s unique adaptations, inspired by its maritime heritage and multicultural influences.

In this hands-on experience, you'll learn about the intricate craftsmanship behind wooden masks, vividly painted with symbolic colors that play a vital role in rituals, theater, and healing ceremonies. Discover the folklore and spiritual traditions woven into these masks, along with Negombo’s distinctive touch on this ancient art form.

Perfect for art enthusiasts and cultural explorers, this workshop offers a rare chance to connect with Sri Lanka’s rich heritage through storytelling, creativity, and the joy of crafting your very own traditional mask.
