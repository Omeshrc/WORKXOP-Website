Paint Your Ocean

The "Paint Your Ocean" workshop offers a guided painting experience inspired by the vibrant coastal life of Negombo, Sri Lanka. Participants will explore the cultural significance of the ocean in Negombo's fishing community, gaining insights into local history and artistry. Led by skilled artisans, the workshop introduces various painting techniques, such as brushwork, color blending, and the use of traditional coastal motifs like fishing boats and marine life—to help participants create their own personal depictions of the ocean.

The session concludes with a community sharing activity where participants reflect on their artwork, fostering a deeper understanding of Negombo's coastal culture. Each participant will leave with a completed painting as a unique keepsake.

  • Incorporate local motifs, such as fishing boats and marine life, into your artwork.
  • Take part in a reflection session where participants share and discuss their artwork.
  • Take home a unique painting as a memento of the experience.
