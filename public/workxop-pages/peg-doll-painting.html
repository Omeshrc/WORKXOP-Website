Peg Doll Painting Workshop

The Peg Doll Painting Workshop offers a hands-on experience where participants can design and paint wooden peg dolls inspired by the coastal culture and folklore of Negombo, Sri Lanka. This workshop combines creativity and storytelling, allowing participants to craft peg dolls that capture elements of Sri Lanka’s fishing heritage and vibrant local narratives. Guided by experienced artisans, participants will learn various painting techniques and cultural motifs, creating a unique keepsake that reflects the spirit of Negombo’s fishing community.

This two-hour workshop includes an introduction to the cultural significance of peg dolls, focusing on their role in storytelling and as symbols of heritage. Each participant will transform their doll into a colorful representation of local legends, fishermen, or scenes of coastal life. The experience concludes with a community sharing session, where participants display their creations and discuss the inspirations behind their designs, fostering cultural appreciation and personal connections.
